Imposter Syndrome

Imposter syndrome is the internal belief that you are not as capable as you thought you were. It leads to feelings of self-doubt despite your education, accomplishments, and experience.

Several women in male-dominant fields such as engineering have experienced imposter syndrome after facing mysoginy and derogatory colleagues in their field.
